My Favorite:

Video Games

Introduction

My favorite video games are based on story, not from gameplay. Personally, I love stories, I also like watching things, and as someone who doesn't really care about movies and TV shows sometimes, video games with stories are my jam. Most of my exposure to games are from watching youtubers play them, or just from watching a walkthrough. Sure, I do enjoy seeing how the mechanics work and occasionally try them out myself (when I can afford said games), but there's just something so alluring about how the video game shows its story to you. The cutscenes are cinematic a lot of times and it's almost like watching a movie, but video games are not movies and that's what makes it special to me.

Sometimes you can even see more lore tidbits from collecting notes, tapes, or even just exploring and talking to different characters. I guess the more movie and TV show adjacent version of this would be web series, and I also enjoy web series, but back to video games. The characters themselves are something I also enjoy in games. Gameplay itself tells us about characters sometimes, how they move when you are controlling them, their voice lines when something crazy happens, I love those little details. Here, I will mention some games and my favorite characters from each one.(The listed games are not in any particular order.)
