The Ace Attorney Game Series

Introduction

Ace Attorney is a visual novel adventure game series created by Capcom. It follows the protagonist, defense attorney Phoenix Wright, and his court adventures, where he battles to save his clients. It includes 6 mainline games: the Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney Trilogy and the Apollo Justice: Ace Attorney Trilogy. Plus 4 spin-off games: Ace Attorney Investigations Collection, where our protagonist is prosecutor Miles Edgeworth, and The Great Ace Attorney Chronicles, where our protagonist is Phoenix Wright’s ancestor, Ryunosuke Naruhodo.

I have either played or watched gameplays of all the games except for The Great Ace Attorney Chronicles, but that doesn't mean I can't list down my favorites. I will talk about some of my favorite games in the series, the cases in it which I liked the most, and my favorite characters from them. (The listed games are not in any specific order, I just like them)

There will probably be spoilers.

Phoenix Wright: Trials and Tribulations

"Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney – Trials and Tribulations is a visual novel adventure video game developed and published by Capcom. It was originally released for the Game Boy Advance in 2004 in Japan, and has since been released for several platforms, including a Nintendo DS version that was released in 2007 in Japan and North America and in 2008 in Europe.

The story follows defense attorneys Phoenix Wright and Mia Fey, who defend their clients in five episodes. Among other characters are Phoenix's assistant and Mia's sister Maya, her cousin Pearl, and prosecutor Godot."

I liked this game a lot, gameplay-wise? It's pretty much the same as the classic Phoenix Wright games, story-wise? It's honestly the best in the trilogy. The episodes of the game were not in chronological order, so you had to put them together yourself.

Episode 1: Turnabout Memories

Here we play 4 years before the actual setting of Trials and Tribulations. Our main character in the case is none other than Mia Fey, and she is here to defend her client, Phoenix Wright!
